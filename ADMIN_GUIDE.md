# AgroPure Website - Admin Guide

## Quick Start

Your website content is managed through **Sanity Studio** - a simple visual editor.

**Admin URL:** `https://rsmagri.sanity.studio/`

---

## Logging In

1. Go to your Sanity Studio URL
2. Click **"Log in"**
3. Use your email/password or Google account
4. You're in!

## How to Edit Content

1. **Click on any content type** in the left sidebar
2. **Select an existing item** or click **"+ Create"** for new
3. **Make your changes** in the editor
4. **Click "Publish"** (green button, top right)

> **Changes go live within 1-2 minutes!**

---

## Uploading Images

1. Click on any image field
2. **Drag & drop** your image, or click to browse
3. Recommended sizes:
   - Hero background: 1920x1080 pixels
   - Product images: 800x600 pixels
   - Logo: 200x200 pixels (PNG with transparency)
